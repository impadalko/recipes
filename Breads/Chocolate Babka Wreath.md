## Ingredients

* 500 g flour
* ½ cup warm milk
* 100 g butter, at room temperature
* 70 g granulated sugar
* 5 g instant yeast
* 2 eggs
* 1 tsp salt
* Chocolate spread and cracked nuts

## Instructions

1. In a large kneading bowl, mix the flour, sugar, eggs, salt, milk, and yeast. Knead for five minutes.
2. Add the room-temperature butter and continue kneading until a soft, smooth dough forms.
3. Cover the dough and refrigerate overnight for its first fermentation.
4. Lightly flour your counter and roll the dough out into a large rectangle.
5. Spread the chocolate evenly over the dough and sprinkle with the cracked nuts.
6. Roll the dough tightly into a log, starting from the long edge.
7. Cut the log completely in half lengthwise, exposing the chocolate layers.
8. Twist the two halves together, then bring the ends together to form a circular wreath shape.
9. Let the shaped wreath ferment for 15–20 minutes.
10. Bake on a preheated oven at 180°C for 30–35 minutes.
