## Ingredients

* 120 g flour
* 25 g sugar 
* 2 ½ tsp baking powder
* ½ tsp salt
* 2 very ripe bananas, mashed (leave some chunks)
* 240 ml whole milk
* 1 large egg
* 1 tsp vanilla extract
* butter
## Instructions

1. Whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl, then set aside.
2. Mash the bananas in a separate bowl, leaving some chunks for texture. Add the milk, egg, and vanilla.
3. Pour the wet mixture into the dry mixture and stir until just combined. A few lumps are okay.
4. Place a large pan or griddle over medium heat. Lightly grease with butter. 
5. Working in batches, add about 1 scoop of batter to the pan per pancake. Cook for 2 to 3 minutes, until golden brown on the bottom and bubbles have formed all over the surface. 
6. Flip and cook for an additional minute or until golden brown on the bottom.
7. Continue cooking pancakes, adding more butter to the skillet between each batch.
