## Ingredients

* 395 g sweetened condensed milk
* 400 g cream
* 1 packet pineapple-flavored powdered drink mix
* 100 g shredded coconut (unsweetened)
* Milk
* Cornstarch cookies (biscoito maisena) or ladyfingers
* Extra shredded coconut (for garnish)

## Instructions

1. In a bowl, thoroughly mix the condensed milk, table cream, and powdered pineapple drink mix until the mixture thickens.
2. Fold the 100 g of shredded coconut into the thickened cream.
3. Spread a thin layer of the cream mixture on the bottom of a serving dish.
4. Briefly dip the cookies into the milk, then arrange them in a single layer over the cream.
5. Repeat the layers, alternating between the cream mixture and the milk-dipped cookies, ending with a final layer of the cream.
6. Garnish the top with extra shredded coconut.
7. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
