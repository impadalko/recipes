## Ingredients

* 400 g potatoes
* 120 g flour
* 1 tsp baking powder
* 1 tsp salt
* 1 egg
* 120 ml milk
* Green onions
* Butter
* Cheese and cold cuts

## Instructions

1. Peel and cut the potatoes, then boil them in water for 20 minutes until soft.
2. Turn on the waffle iron.
3. Drain the potatoes and mash them in a large bowl.
4. Add the flour, baking powder, salt, egg, chopped green onions, and milk. Mix everything until it forms a thick batter.
5. Spread some butter in the waffle iron.
6. Spread a layer of the potato batter onto the waffle iron.
7. Place the cheese and cold cuts over the batter.
8. Cover with another layer of batter.
9. Close the waffle iron and cook until golden brown and crispy.
