## Ingredients

* 3 apples
* 100 g unsalted butter, room temperature
* 100 g granulated sugar
* 25 g light brown sugar
* 2 large eggs
* 1 tsp vanilla extract
* 3 tbsp dark rum
* 120 g all-purpose flour
* 1 tsp baking powder
* 1 tsp salt
## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
2. In a small bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
3. In a large mixing bowl, beat the butter and sugars together until light and fluffy.
4. Mix in the eggs one at a time, then add the vanilla extract and rum.
5. Gently add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. 
6. Fold the cubed apples into the batter.
7. Pour the mixture into the prepared pan, spreading it out into an even layer.
8. Dust some granulated sugar on top.
9. Bake for 35–40 minutes.
