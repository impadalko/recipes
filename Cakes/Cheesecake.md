## Ingredients

**For the Crust:**
* 150 g biscuits
* 100 g butter

**For the Filling:**
* 600 g cream cheese
* 100 g sugar
* 3 eggs
* 200 g cream
* 1 tsp vanilla extract

**For the Jam:**
* 300 g frozen strawberries
* 100 g sugar
* Juice of 1 lemon

**For the Topping:**
* Fresh red berries for decoration

## Instructions

1. Crush the biscuits and mix them with the butter until it forms a crumbly texture that holds its shape when squeezed in your hand.
2. Press the mixture firmly into the bottom of a springform pan.
3. In a bowl, beat the cream cheese. Add the sugar, eggs, heavy cream, and vanilla. Blend well until the mixture is completely smooth.
4. Pour the filling over the prepared crust.
5. Bake at 180°C for 45–50 minutes. The edges should be set, but the center can still jiggle slightly.
6. While the cheesecake bakes, prepare the jam by cooking the frozen strawberries, sugar, and lemon juice in a saucepan over medium heat. Let it reduce until it becomes a glossy, fluid jam. Transfer to a bowl to cool completely.
7. Refrigerate the cheesecake until it is completely cold, then carefully unmold it.
8. Top the cold cheesecake with the cooled strawberry jam and decorate with the fresh red berries before serving.
