Original recipe: https://panelinha.com.br/receita/bolo-de-laranja
## Ingredients

* 3 oranges
* 2 eggs
* 1 cup neutral oil
* 1 ½ cups refined sugar
* 2 tbsp powdered sugar
* 240 g all-purpose flour
	* You can replace up to 30 g of flour with oat bran for extra fiber
* 2 tsp baking powder
* 1 pinch of salt
## Instructions

**For the Cake:**
1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
2. Cut 2 oranges into quarters, remove the white core and the seeds, and cut the pieces smaller. Place them into a blender.
3. Add the eggs to the blender along with the oil and refined sugar. Blend well until smooth.
4. In a medium bowl, mix the flour, oat bran, baking powder and salt. 
5. Add the blended liquid into the bowl, gently mixing with a wire whisk until incorporated.
6. Pour the batter into a 20 cm by 30 cm pan and bake.
7. Squeeze the juice of the remaining orange and mix it with the powdered sugar, gently spread the glaze with a brush on top of the still hot cake.
